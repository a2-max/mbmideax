AR / VR Track

Participants in the AR/VR track will explore the immersive world of augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technologies. They can develop innovative applications and experiences ranging from simulations and training tools to immersive experiences and interactive virtual environments.

Game Development Track

The Games track invites participants to unleash their creativity and passion for game development. From casual mobile games to complex multiplayer experiences, participants can design and develop captivating games that entertain, inspire, and challenge players across different platforms and genres.

IOT Track

In the IoT track, participants will dive into the world of connected devices and smart systems. They can create innovative IoT solutions that leverage sensor data, cloud computing, and real-time analytics to solve real-world problems, optimize processes, and improve efficiency.

AI / ML Track

The AI/ML track provides participants with an opportunity to explore the cutting-edge fields of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML). Participants can develop intelligent applications and algorithms that analyze data, make predictions, automate tasks, and enhance decision-making across various domains. They can also delve into the new world of generative AI for creating top-notch solutions.

Blockchain Track

Participants in the Blockchain track will delve into the decentralized world of blockchain technology. They can create innovative solutions that leverage blockchain's distributed ledger to enhance transparency, security, and trust in applications.

Web App Track

The Web Applications track focuses on web development and cloud computing technologies. Participants can build responsive web applications, progressive web apps (PWAs), and cloud-native solutions that deliver seamless user experiences, scalability, and performance across different devices and browsers.

Open Track

The track provides participants with the freedom to unleash their creativity and innovation without constraints. Whether it's creating an app for a smartwatch, designing a futuristic IoT device, experimenting with robotics, or pushing the boundaries of wearable technology, the sky's the limit in this category. Participants are encouraged to think outside the box.
