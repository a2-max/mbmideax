About Us

MBM IdeaX – ICT for Indigenous Languages is a 48-hour on-site hackathon that aims to bring together young people from all over Nepal to generate innovative and creative solutions for preserving and promoting indigenous languages using ICT. This hackathon is a part of the International Decade of Indigenous Languages (IDIL) mission to save, preserve, and promote Indigenous languages that are at the verge of loss. The event is organized by Madan Bhandari Memorial College in collaboration with UNESCO Nepal.

FAQS

A hackathon is an event where people come to learn, network and enjoy while developing a solution for a presented problem.

IdeaX aims to bring together young people from all over Nepal to generate ideas and solutions for preserving and promoting indigenous languages using ICT.

Hackathon on Indigenous Languages promotes the preservation, learning, accessibility, and promotion of endangered indigenous languages through innovative technical solutions.

Preserving, promoting, and revitalizing indigenous languages is crucial because these languages are integral to the cultural identity and heritage of indigenous communities. They contain valuable knowledge, traditions, and ways of life that have been passed down through generations. By protecting and promoting these languages, we can help ensure their survival and contribute to the cultural diversity and richness of our world.
